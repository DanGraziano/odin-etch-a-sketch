Etch-A-Sketch

How to play:

  • Click the 'Play Game' button below to begin the game
  • You will be asked to choose a number between 10 and 65.
  • This will determine the size of the board (e.g. 10 x 10, 65 x 65)
  • Next simply hover your mouse over the board to begin drawing
  • Want to start over? Click the 'Reset Game' button
